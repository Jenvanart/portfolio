Branding & Logo

[re]claim heritage textile co.

[re]claim heritage textile co. focuses on bespoke clothing fabrication. Each garment is handcrafted from sourced vintage fabrics, telling a unique story while advocating individuality and self-expression. The brand's identity was built on typography, colour, patterns and lifestyle photography. Logo and visual assets were designed to humanize the brand and message, tell a story, and create a personal connection with the audience.
