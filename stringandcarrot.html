String & Carrot

String&Carrot is a Toronto-based food access initiative focused on providing better food security to areas of Toronto where affordable, accessible produce is not readily available. Produce is packed into reusable, robust, gable boxes, perfectly designed to fit in food delivery messenger bags. Food messengers can participate in the program, delivering along routes they are already travelling. Patterns, simple illustrations and bright colours support the initiative's goals and focus on providing eco-friendly, positive food security solutions.
