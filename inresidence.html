Editorial

In-Residence Magazine

Much like an art residency, In-Residence seeks to collaborate with artists to support their practice and uplift the fine arts community. A specific emphasis is made on celebrating both traditional artistic methods and more modern artistic approaches. The interior of the magazine reads like a gallery: clean, well-lit, designed to highlight and focus on the art it houses, not distract. In contrast, the cover purposely abstracts a classical painting, creating visual tension that plays off the theme of this issue 'Show Your Colours'.
