ERA Café

ERA Café celebrates the legacy of cafés as vibrant hubs of creativity and exchange. Nestled in Toronto's Distillery District, ERA Café is a perfect place to meet before a day of cultural exploration. With a nod to the district's artistic flair, ERA serves as a luxury coffee destination, gallery, and event space. A repeatable geometric pattern is woven through the website, branding and packaging design. This pattern creates a cohesive atmosphere within the branding that easily adapts to various mediums, creating a robust brand image.
