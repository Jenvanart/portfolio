Helmets Through Time

This rental catalogue features helmets from the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Armoury Collection. Relevant artworks surrounding the subject of war, battle, or armour are inlaid throughout the booklet, complementing the helmets for rent. These images add historical reference, texture, and visual interest to the catalogue spread. Classic typography is modified to create a highly graphic, but restrained direction for the type-heavy interior spreads. The layout, typography, colour palette and interior imagery bring high-end and contemporary energy to the catalogue.
